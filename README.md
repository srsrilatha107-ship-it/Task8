# VPN Privacy and Secure Communication

## Objective
Understand the role of VPNs in protecting privacy and providing secure communication.

## Tools
- ProtonVPN (Free Tier) / Windscribe Free  
- Web Browser (for IP verification)

## Steps
1. Chose a reputable free VPN service and signed up.  
2. Downloaded and installed the VPN client.  
3. Connected to a VPN server (nearest/any location).  
4. Verified IP change via [whatismyipaddress.com](https://www.whatismyipaddress.com).  
5. Browsed websites to confirm traffic encryption.  
6. Disconnected VPN and compared browsing speed/IP.  
7. Researched VPN encryption and privacy features.  

## Benefits
- Hides real IP and location  
- Encrypts internet traffic  
- Protects against eavesdropping (especially on public Wi-Fi)  
- Bypasses geo-restrictions  

## Limitations
- Free VPNs may limit servers and speed  
- Possible logging with untrusted providers  
- Slightly slower browsing due to encryption overhead  

## Deliverables
- Screenshots:  
  - VPN signup/installation  
  - VPN connection status  
  - IP before/after VPN  
  - Browsing with VPN enabled  

## Conclusion
VPNs enhance privacy and security by encrypting communication and masking IP addresses. However, free VPNs come with limitations, so choosing a trusted provider is essential.
